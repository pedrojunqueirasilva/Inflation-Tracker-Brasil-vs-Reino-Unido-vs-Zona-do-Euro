# How to Present This Project in Internship Interviews

1. **Start with the business question**
   - “I wanted to compare inflation and cost-of-living dynamics across three major economies to understand policy trade-offs in real conditions.”

2. **Explain data credibility first**
   - “I used public, trustworthy API data from the World Bank and structured the project so it can be extended with central-bank and statistical-office APIs.”

3. **Describe your workflow clearly**
   - “I built a modular pipeline: ingestion, cleaning/harmonisation, panel construction, insight generation, and dashboard specification.”

4. **Show analytical judgement**
   - “I handled unit and timing comparability, created an exchange-rate index, and interpreted outcomes in terms of inflation pressure, labour market resilience, and growth recovery.”

5. **Highlight limitations and next steps**
   - “Annual harmonisation improves comparability but smooths intra-year dynamics. Next step is to add monthly CPI and policy-rate series from ONS/Eurostat/BCB APIs.”

6. **Close with impact**
   - “The project demonstrates I can translate macroeconomic complexity into decision-useful analytics and communicate insights for non-technical stakeholders.”
